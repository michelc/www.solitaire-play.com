Golf Solitaire Game (or play Klondike Solitaire)

Your goal is to win! You win the game only when you move all cards to the foundation (the pile in the bottom right corner). As the win rate is very low, the fun is to play as quickly as possible. Open your eyes!

Cards are shuffled and 35 are dealt face up on the tableau (the 7 piles in the top of the screen). The remaining 16 cards forms the stock (bottom left) and the last card starts the foundation.

  • You can send any card from the tableau that is greater or lesser than the current card to the foundation, regardless of suit.
  • Sequences may turn the corner with Kings played on Aces and Aces played on Kings (otherwise it's quite impossible to win).

When no more cards from the tableau can be transferred to the foundation, you can deal a card from the stock to the foundation and the game go on. And when the stock is empty, then you have lost. One more time...

How to play Free Golf Solitaire

Golf is an easy HTML5 and JavaScript card game. No drag & drop, just speed and some clicks:

  • Click on a card from the tableau to move it to the foundation
  • Click on the stock to draw a new card
  • Right-click (or tap and hold) to move all possible cards to the foundations

Golf Solitaire online game

Simply click the top left title to play a new solitaire game, or use one of the menu commands:

  • GAME: Play a new game of solitaire (or press F2) at any time, even in the middle of a game.
  • HINT: Get some help to find your next best move (or press H).
  • UNDO: Back up one move (or press Control+Z). You can continue to undo until you reach the beginning of the game.
