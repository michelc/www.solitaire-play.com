Pyramid Solitaire Game (or play Pyramid Turn Three)

Your goal is to win! You win the game when you move all cards to the foundation (the empty pile in the upper right corner). You fill the foundation by removing a pair of cards from the tableau. Open your eyes!

At the beginning of the game, cards are shuffled and 28 are dealt face up in a pyramid shape on the tableau. The remaining 16 cards forms the stock (top left) and the game start with one card turned from the deck into the waste pile.

You can pair two cards where ranks add up to 13 (regardless of suit) to transfer them to the foundation. With a rank of 13, Kings are removed individually.

You can make pair with any card:

  • That is free with no other card lying on top of it.
  • That will be made free by removing the first card.
  • That is on top of the waste pile.

Once all possible pairs are removed, you turn one card from the stock to the waste pile and the game go on. When the stock become empty, the cards from the waste pile are reused.

How to play Free Pyramid Solitaire

Pyramid is a basic HTML5 and JavaScript card game. No drag & drop, just patience, observation and some clicks:

  • Click on a card from the tableau or the waste to start a pair
  • Click on another card to move the pair to the foundation
  • Click on the stock to draw one more card
  • Right-click (or tap and hold) to move all possible pairs to the foundations

Pyramid Solitaire online game
