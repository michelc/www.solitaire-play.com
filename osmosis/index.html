Osmosis Solitaire Game (or play Klondike Solitaire)

Your goal is to win! You win the game only when you move all cards to the foundations (the 4 horizontal piles in the center). The win rate is pretty low, but it's an interesting and different solitaire which teach patience and attention.

Cards are shuffled and 16 go in 4 reserve piles of 4 cards into a column along the left. Then one card is dealt to the right of the top reserve pile to forms the first foundation. The remaining 35 cards forms the stock (top left) and the game starts with one card turned into the waste pile.

  • Each foundation starts with the same rank than the first one
  • Foundations build in suit, regardless of order
  • Any card of the same suit can go to the first foundation
  • You can play a card to a lower foundation only if a card with the same rank is already in the foundation above (the “osmosis rule”)
  • Only the top card from reserve or waste piles is playable

When all moves are done, you turn one card from the stock to the waste. When you're out of stock, the waste flip over.

How to play Free Osmosis Solitaire

Osmosis is an easy HTML5 and JavaScript card game. No drag & drop, just pay attention to not miss a possible play:

  • Click on a card from the reserve to move it to an empty pile or a foundation
  • Click on the stock to draw one new card
  • Right-click (or tap and hold) to move all possible cards to the foundations

Osmosis Solitaire online game

Simply click the top left title to play a new solitaire game, or use one of the menu commands:

  • GAME: Play a new game of solitaire (or press F2) at any time, even in the middle of a game.
  • HINT: Get some help to find your next best move (or press H).
  • UNDO: Back up one move (or press Control+Z). You can continue to undo until you reach the beginning of the game.
