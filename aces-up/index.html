Aces Up Solitaire Game (or play Klondike Solitaire)

Your goal is to win! You win the game only when you discard all cards to the foundation (the place in the bottom right corner), leaving only each suit of Aces at the top of the tableau piles.

At the beginning of the game, cards are shuffled and 4 are dealt face up on the tableau (the 4 piles in the top of the screen). The remaining 48 cards forms the stock in the upper right-hand side.

  • You can only play cards at the top of a tableau pile.
  • If there are 2 top cards of the same suit, you can discard the lower to the foundation.
  • Ranking goes from 2, 3, 4, ..., 10, Jack, Queen, King and Ace.
  • You can move any top cards to an empty tableau pile.

When there are no more cards to move to an empty pile or to the foundation, you deal four cards from the stock to the top of each pile on the tableau.

The game ends when all cards from the stock have been played. You won if the only cards left on the tableau are the four Aces.

How to play Free Aces Up Solitaire

Aces Up is a very simple HTML5 and JavaScript card game. No drag & drop, mostly luck and you get a fun and relaxing game.

  • Click on a card from the tableau to move it to an empty pile or the foundation
  • Click on the stock to draw 4 new cards
  • Right-click (or tap and hold) to move all possible cards to the foundation

Aces Up Solitaire online game

Simply click the top left title to play a new solitaire game, or use one of the menu commands:

  • GAME: Play a new game of solitaire (or press F2) at any time, even in the middle of a game.
  • HINT: Get some help to find your next best move (or press H).
  • UNDO: Back up one move (or press Control+Z). You can continue to undo until you reach the beginning of the game.
