    How to play?

    Like Boggle, the goal of the game is to find as many words as possible among the 16 letters in the grid. Use these letters to write new words and validate them with the enter key (bottom right).

    Rules

    • The grid cells used to form a word must touch each other, either horizontally (left / right), vertically (up / down) or diagonally.
    • No grid cell may be used more than once in a word.
    • A word must contain at least 4 letters.

    Scoring

    • 4-letter words are worth 1 point each.
    • Longer words earn 1 point per letter.

    Valid words

    • The playable words are taken from Common English Lexicon (CEL). This list is used as is and is not checked, revised or completed.
    • Verbs and conjugations are allowed.
    • Proper nouns are not accepted.
    • Each game has a word of at least 8 letters. Well, almost in the beta period...
