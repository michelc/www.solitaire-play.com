Canfield Turn Three Game (or play Canfield Solitaire)

Your goal is to win! You win the game when you move all cards to the foundations (the 4 piles in the upper right corner).

Cards are shuffled and 13 go face down to the reserve (the vertical pile) with the top card face up. Then one card is dealt to the first foundation and into each tableau pile. The remaining 34 cards forms the stock (top left) and the game starts with three cards turned from the stock into the waste pile.

  • Each foundation starts with the same rank than the first one
  • Foundations build up by suit, wrapping from King to Ace if necessary
  • Tableau piles build down by alternating color, wrapping from Ace to King if necessary
  • Only the top card from tableau pile is playable, but you can move a full pile
  • An empty tableau pile is filled with the reserve
  • When the reserve runs out, any playable card can fill a free pile

When all moves are done, you turn three cards from the stock to the waste. When you're out of stock, the waste flip over.

How to play Free Canfield Turn Three

Canfield Turn Three is an easy to learn HTML5 and JavaScript card game, with a gameplay similar to Klondike Solitaire:

  • Use drag & drop to move cards from one pile to another
  • Click on the stock to draw three new cards
  • Double-click on a card to add it to a foundation
  • Right-click (or tap and hold) to autoplay all possible cards to the foundations

Canfield Turn Three online game

Simply click the top left title to play a new solitaire game, or use one of the menu commands:

  • GAME: Play a new game of solitaire (or press F2) at any time, even in the middle of a game.
  • HINT: Get some help to find your next best move (or press H).
  • UNDO: Back up one move (or press Control+Z). You can continue to undo until you reach the beginning of the game.
