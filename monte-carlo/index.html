Monte Carlo Solitaire Game (or play Klondike Solitaire)

Your goal is to win! You win the game when you move all cards to the foundation (the empty pile in the upper right corner). You fill the foundation by discarding a pair of cards from the tableau.

At the begining of the game, all cards are shuffled and 25 are dealt on the tableau into a five by five grid. The remaining 27 cards form the stock in the bottom right pile and you are ready to play.

You can pair cards of the same rank (regardless of suit) to transfer them to the foundation. The two cards just have to be adjacent horizontally, vertically or diagonally to be pick up.

Once all possible pairs are removed, you click the stock to collapse all empty spaces from the tableau and deal cards from the stock until all 5 rows are filled again. When the stock is empty, you can always click it to collapse empty cards and continue the game. You lose when you can't build a new pair after a redeal.

How to play Free Monte Carlo Solitaire

Monte Carlo is a basic HTML5 and JavaScript matching card patience. No drag & drop, just quiet, concentration and clicks:

  • Click on a card from the tableau to start a pair
  • Click on another card from the tableau to move the pair to the foundation
  • Click on the stock to collapse then redeal cards
  • Right-click (or tap and hold) to move all possible cards to the foundation

Monte Carlo Solitaire online game

Simply click the top left title to play a new solitaire game, or use one of the menu commands:

  • GAME: Play a new game of solitaire (or press F2) at any time, even in the middle of a game.
  • HINT: Get some help to find a valid pair of cards (or press H).
  • UNDO: Back up one move (or press Control+Z). You can continue to undo until you reach the beginning of the game.
