Solitaire / Online Solitaire Games

Solitaire-Play is a collection of great solitaire card games to play online. Solitaire games are always fun and relaxing to play, but also challenging and rewarding. Solitaire-Play are HTML5 and JavaScript solitaire games. You can play wherever and whenever you want for free on many devices: desktop, laptop, phone or tablet.

Each solitaire game come with an unlimited “undo” function, which lets you try different ways to play your game. There is also an “auto-play” feature that quickly build cards from the tableau to foundations. And all Solitaire-Play games give access to “hints”, usefull to learn how to play solitaire games and be sure to never miss any move.

Free Solitaire Games:

Klondike Solitaire

Probably the best known and most popular solitaire. Easy, fun and winnable with a little patience and strategy.

The three turn Klondike solitaire game. A more challenging way to play this classic solitaire, but still easy and fun to try.

Also known as Idiot's Delight, this solitaire card game is simple but compelling. Don't quit till you have won at least once.

Alaska is a Russian Solitaire variation where you can build tableau up or down by rank and by suit. So it's easier to win?

A challenging combination of Klondike, Scorpion, and Yukon. The fact that all the cards are face up doesn't make the game easier.

An easy variation of Australian Patience. With one redraw, you can expect to win every game, but it's still thrilling to play.

Named after Richard A. Canfield, gambler and casino owner, this old solitaire game is very popular but requires a lot of planning.

Some people think it's too easy to win when you turn only one card at a time. Now they can play three turn Canfield solitaire game.

Cruel Solitaire

The perfect name for this simple but addictive solitaire. It takes a lot of time, concentration and perseverance to win.

FreeCell Solitaire

Surely the most popular solitaire, with a puzzle-like logic. Some games require cleverness, others are tricky, so it's always a challenge.

Golf Solitaire

A fast and fun solitaire card game. Easy to play, and very exciting, but you'll have to show some luck and patience to win.

Monte Carlo Solitaire

A catchy and relaxing matching solitaire card game. Start to play, keep trying and you'll be successfull to win.

Pyramid Solitaire

A simple and captivating solitaire card game. Start to play, match pairs that add up to lucky 13 and clear the pyramid of cards!

Pyramid Turn Three

Russian Solitaire

Russian Solitaire is a thrilling variation of Yukon which requires a lot more attention and strategy to win, luckily.

Thoughtful Solitaire

Yukon Solitaire
